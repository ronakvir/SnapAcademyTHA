At the Tree Farm

We never pretend to be the cheapest, but do have the best quality Christmas Trees. We carry Nobles, Nordmanns, and Douglas Firs. We are a full service lot. We will help you pick the best type of tree for your home, fresh cut it and secure it on your vehicle. If you like your tree to have a “fresh fallen snow” look; we have many flocked trees to choose from. We also carry gorgeous fresh garland and wreaths. We are open daily November 25th – December 21st 2022 or Until Sold Out

  • Noble Fir: Simply the best Christmas tree grown anywhere in the world! Noble Firs are native to the Cascade Range and Coast Range mountains of extreme northwest California and western Oregon and Washington. Today Noble Firs are the most popular Christmas tree in California and the western United States

  • Nordmann Fir: Originated from the Caucasus Mountains, south and east of the Black Sea in the Republic of Georgia and northern Turkey. The Nordmann Fir has been popular in Europe across the ages and are gaining even more popularity in the United States.

  • Douglas Fir: The traditional Christmas tree species from Oregon, and has been produced in the Pacific Northwest since the 1920’s, when most Christmas trees came from the forest. The Douglas Fir still remains one of the most commonly used Christmas tree species in the United States.
